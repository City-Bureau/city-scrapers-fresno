EXETER IN A NUTSHELL

Exeter is an incorporated city of just under 11,000 population, located in California’s San Joaquin Valley. Situated just south of Highway 198, approximately 15 miles east of Highway 99, Exeter lies near the foothills at the base of the Sierra Nevada mountains, on the path to Sequoia National Park.

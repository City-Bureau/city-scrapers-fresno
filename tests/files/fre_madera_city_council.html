City Council Meetings & Agendas

All City Council meetings, except Closed Sessions, are open to the public. Closed Sessions are held to discuss litigation, personnel matters, claims, and sensitive real estate negotiations.

Posting of Agendas

Agendas for regularly scheduled meetings are posted on the bulletin board at City Hall and on the City of Madera website at least 72 hours before the scheduled meeting. Special meeting agendas are posted at least 24 hours before the scheduled meeting. GC 54954.2(a)(1).

Meeting Dates

Regular meetings of the Madera City Council are held on the first and third Wednesday of each month at 6:00 p.m. Meetings are held in the Council Chambers at City Hall located at 205 W. 4th St., Madera California 93637.

 

Item Type

Regular Meeting: RM
Special Meeting: SM
Joint Meeting: JM
Late Distribution: LD

Questions regarding the meeting agenda may be directed to the Office of the City Clerk at (559) 661-5405 or by visiting City Hall.

2022 Meeting Schedule

  • Wednesday, January 19, 2022 (Special Session -Tentative-ZOOM)
  • Wednesday, February 16, 2022 (Election of Officers)
  • Wednesday, March 16, 2022 (Special Session -Tentative-ZOOM)
  • Wednesday, April 13, 2022
  • Wednesday, May 11. 2022 (Special Session -Tentative-ZOOM)
  • Wednesday, June 8, 2022 (Budget Hearing)
  • Wednesday, July 20, 2022 (Special Session -Tentative-ZOOM)
  • Wednesday, August 17, 2022
  • Wednesday, September 14, 2022 (Special Session -Tentative-ZOOM)
  • Wednesday, October 12, 2022
  • Wednesday, November 16, 2022 (Special Session -Tentative-ZOOM)
  • Wednesday, December 14, 2022

The 2022 meeting dates for the Fresno County Transportation Authority Board are scheduled for 9:00 a.m. and will be held in the Fresno Council of Government’s Ash Conference Room located at 2035 Tulare Street, Suite 201, 2nd Floor, Fresno, California. All interested parties will be notified of any change to this schedule. (Due to COVID-19, the meeting location and meeting type could change). Parking for Board Members and Staff will be made available in the underground parking facilities off of Van Ness and Tulare. All participants must bring their parking stubs to the meeting for a parking validation stamp so they will not be charged for parking. You may reach us at 600-3282 (FCTA) or through email at denise@thefcta.com with any questions or concerns you may have.

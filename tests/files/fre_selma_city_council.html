Council Meetings & Agendas

Meetings of the Selma City Council are held on the 1st and 3rd Mondays of each month (Tuesdays if there is a holiday) at 6:00 p.m. in the Council chambers located at 1710 Tucker Street, Selma, California. All meetings are open to the public and citizens are encouraged to attend.
